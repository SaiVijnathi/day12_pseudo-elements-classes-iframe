West Indian Dishes

Pav Bhaji

Originating in Maharashtra, Pav Bhaji is a spicy vegetable mash served with buttered bread rolls (pav). The bhaji is a mix of mashed potatoes, tomatoes, peas, and a blend of spices, garnished with fresh onions, cilantro, and a squeeze of lemon. It's a beloved street food in Mumbai.

Dhokla

A savory snack from Gujarat, dhokla is a steamed, spongy cake made from fermented rice and chickpea flour. It’s slightly tangy, soft, and often garnished with mustard seeds, green chilies, and fresh coriander. Served with chutney, it's a light, healthy snack or breakfast option.
